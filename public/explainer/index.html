Instructions

In each demo page, you will be presented with 3 boxes that contain the content rendered from:

  • Same-origin (your site)
  • Trusted cross-origin in an iframe
  • Untrusted cross-origin in an iframe

The above boxes behave different based on how the Permissions-Policy header was constructed. In the demo, we take a look at 5 different ways that the header can be cosntructed to control the feature.

  • All origins present on the page including every iframe
  • Some origins that are listed in the header allowlist
  • Same-origin only
  • No origins
  • Navigated iframe

When you visit each demo, you will be presented with a prompt for geolocation access. Your geolocation data is not transmitted from your device to anywhere, and your data is kept private.

To reset the demo, find the lock icon in the left side of the address bar, and click "Reset permissions".
